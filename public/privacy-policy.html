Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 18, 2025

This website ("Platform") serves as a hub connecting Brands and Content Creators (KOLs) for collaboration campaigns. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information. By using this Platform, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information
    • Name, email address, phone number, password, and login details.
  • Profile Information
    • For KOLs: portfolio, niche, number of followers, engagement rate, rates (optional), location.
    • For Brands: company name, industry, campaign budget, and campaign requirements.
  • Campaign Information
    • Campaign briefs, deliverables, budget, progress status, and final results.
  • Platform Interactions
    • Campaign applications, messages/chats, saved/bookmarked campaigns, dashboard activity.
  • Technical Data
    • IP address, device type, browser type, activity logs, and cookies or similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Enable communication between Brands and KOLs (campaigns, chats/DMs, invitations).
  • Provide search and filter features for finding KOLs.
  • Send notifications about new campaigns based on user preferences.
  • Display insights and analytics on campaign performance.
  • Verify accounts and ensure platform security.
  • Send updates about the platform, success stories, collaboration tips, or featured KOLs.
  • Comply with legal obligations and audits.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data to third parties. Information may only be shared in the following cases:

  • Between Users: Brands may view KOL profiles; KOLs may view available campaign details.
  • Service Providers: Third-party partners supporting operations (hosting, payments, analytics), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored on secure servers with technical and administrative safeguards.
  • Account access is protected with passwords/OTP.
  • Encryption and industry-standard security practices are applied to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information.
  • Opt in or out of certain notifications.
  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data usage (with the understanding that this may limit access to some features).

6. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies to improve user experience, such as saving preferences, analyzing traffic, and personalizing content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This Platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. If you are under 18, please do not use this service without parental/guardian supervision.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-platform notifications.
